My favorite Kdramas Of 2024

According to my own ratings

Queen Of Tears

This drama was a solid 10/10.
It did the marriage trope so perfectly.The chemistry between the main leads is something you cant miss.

Lovely Runner

Once again a 10/10 watch.
Looking for something light-hearted?funny and romantic? well lovely runner is just here for you with all those things. it was so funny easy going with thrilling time-travel plot; it was just a rom-com done right

The judge from hell

Rating: 10000/10
never knew i could love a non romantiv thrilling mystery plot show but here we are. It has all the right amount of angst, romance,mystery, chemistry,comedy.This is the peak a drama could ever get for real.
